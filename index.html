About the Book

Rape Free India is not just a publication — it is a battle cry. Drawing on seventeen years of grassroots work, Yogita Bhayana exposes systemic failures that allow violence to persist and calls for policy-driven reform.

This book shifts the focus from outrage to action, offering a blueprint for realizing Mission Rape Free India 2029.
